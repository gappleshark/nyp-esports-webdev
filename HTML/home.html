Our History

NYP Esports was founded in 2022 by a group of passionate students who saw the potential for gaming to go beyond the screen — as a way to bring people together and foster a shared identity.

Over the years, we've built a culture that values passion, inclusivity, and creativity — shaping NYP Esports into a hub where gamers can belong, lead, and level up together.

What We Offer

Whether you're here to unwind or compete, we have something for everyone. We host regular tournaments, community game nights, and exciting watch parties for major esports events.

Member Perks:

  • Access to exclusive events and tournaments
  • Networking opportunities with fellow gamers
  • Workshops and training sessions to improve your skills
  • Discounts on gaming gear and merchandise
